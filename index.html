Common Questions

Should I be a Sole Trader or Limited Company?

Sole trader is simpler and cheaper to run - great for starting out. Limited company gives you liability protection and can be more tax efficient as you grow. Not sure? We'll help you decide!

How long does it take?

Most companies are fully registered within 24-48 hours. Sole trader registration is usually same-day. You'll receive all your documents by email as soon as they're ready.

Do I need an accountant?

Not to get started! We help you set everything up properly. You may want an accountant later for annual accounts, but we'll advise you on that.

What if I make a mistake on the form?

Don't worry! We review everything before submission and will contact you if anything needs clarifying.

What are the Business Plan and Market Study?

These are professional documents tailored specifically for your business. They're perfect for getting bank accounts, attracting investors, or just understanding your market better.

Is my money safe?

Absolutely. We offer a 100% money-back guarantee. If you're not happy for any reason, we'll refund you in full.
